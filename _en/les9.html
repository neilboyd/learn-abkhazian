--- order: 309 title: Lesson 9 subtitle: - "Continuation of the alphabet: new letters and sounds 覕 覕鞋 覕訕" - Work with vocabulary - Consolidation ---

The sound 覕 is a little reminiscent of the southern Russian 袚. The soft variation is 覕鞋, and the rounded variation is 覕訕.

{% include audio.html file="9-1" %} 視 視褜 視訖

Remember these words and expressions!

{% include audio.html file="9-2" %} 邪視斜邪 boat. 袗虂視斜邪 (懈)邪邪虂懈褌. The boat has arrived.
邪虂懈視褜褉邪 (械虂懈視褜褉邪) better. 袗褉懈虂 械虂懈視褜褍锌. It's better.
邪虂懈視褜褏邪褉邪 (械虂懈視褜褏邪褉邪) to get better. 袗褉懈虂 械虂懈視褜褏械懈褌. It's (gotten) better.
邪斜視褜褘虂 leaf
邪斜視褜褘虂褑 diminutive of 邪斜視褜褘.
邪虂視訖視訖邪 strong. 袠邪褉邪虂 写褘視訖視訖芯虂褍锌. He's strong.
懈視訖視訖邪薪褘 strongly. 袗咬褕邪虂 (懈)視訖視訖邪薪褘虂 (懈)邪虂褋褍械懈褌. The wind blows strongly.
邪噎褘虂薪褔 quiet, peaceful, calm. 袗屑褕褘虂薪 懈邪褏褜邪虂 懈噎褘虂薪褔褍锌. The sea is calm today.
邪斜邪虂褉 here, there. 袗斜邪虂褉 邪屑褕褘虂薪 Here's the sea / There's the sea.
邪谐訖邪斜蟹懈虂邪褉邪 health
懈邪褑褘虂 / 械褑褘虂 yesterday

Remember that the inanimate class indicator 懈(邪) disappears in a few places. In such situations we will show it as an optional element in brackets. For example: (懈)邪邪懈褌; (懈)視訖視訖邪薪褘; (懈)邪褋褍械懈褌.

Read the text.

We recommend that you learn all the text and expressions by heart. It will help you to quickly learn enough ready expressions for an active vocabulary.
